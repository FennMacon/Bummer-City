    The Bummer City Open Mic

      Beginning in June of 2015, the Bummer City Historical Society has run monthly (or at least near-monthly) charity open mics. Each mic involves single song/poem/whatever else performances by a series of newcomers and old favorites, broken up in the middle by a featured performing artist. The works of a featured visual artist adorn the space, and both features collaboratively choose an organization or cause that they would like to give money to. Donations are offered up by the audience and are then split 25/25/50 between the two features and their chosen organization or cause. and and


      The mics take place on the 2nd Thursday of each month (except in July & August) at Hostelling International Boston. Sign-ups begin at 7:00pm, performances begin at 7:30pm. All are welcome!


      *Accessibility Note: The hostel is wheelchair accessible.

    About Us

    The Bummer City Historical Society & Civic Engagement Coalition is a Boston-based community of DIY artists and organizers trying to develop intentional, inclusive, civically engaged communities throughout the Greater Boston area.
